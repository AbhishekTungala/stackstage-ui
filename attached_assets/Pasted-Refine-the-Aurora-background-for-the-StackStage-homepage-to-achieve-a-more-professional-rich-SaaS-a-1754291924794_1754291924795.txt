## ✅ 1. Adjust Aurora Background Styling

🎯 Current Issue:
The Aurora gradient is **too bright and harsh**, clashing with content.

### ✔️ Fix:
Use `opacity`, `blur`, and `mix-blend-mode` to soften the Aurora glow.

```tsx
// Aurora.tsx or styled div
<div
  className="absolute inset-0 z-[-1] pointer-events-none"
  style={{
    background: `
      radial-gradient(circle at 30% 30%, rgba(59, 130, 246, 0.2) 0%, transparent 60%),
      radial-gradient(circle at 70% 70%, rgba(124, 58, 237, 0.2) 0%, transparent 60%),
      radial-gradient(circle at 50% 90%, rgba(6, 182, 212, 0.15) 0%, transparent 60%)
    `,
    filter: "blur(100px)",
    opacity: 0.4,
    mixBlendMode: "screen",
  }}
></div>
